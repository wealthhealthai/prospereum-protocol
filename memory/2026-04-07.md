### Critical #1 — recordStakeTime post-snapshot drain
- Bug in StakingVault: no check that epoch hasn't been snapshotted yet
- Attacker stakes silently, skips checkpoints, calls recordStakeTime AFTER snapshotEpoch → 100x drain
- Fix: `require(!epochSnapshotted[epochId])` — 1 line

### Critical #2 — O(V×C) gas ceiling (KNOWN)

### Critical #3 — Flash loan cumS inflation
- live balanceOf scanning in _updateCumS lets anyone transfer PSRE directly to vault, spike cumS, withdraw
- Design flaw: must move to explicit deposit tracking only (no balanceOf scanning)

### High #4/#6 — createVault sandwich/fee tier bypass
- User controls minPsreOut and fee → can route through attacker-controlled pool
- Fix: whitelist fee tiers, enforce minimum PSRE output as % of usdcAmountIn

### High #5 — Cross-epoch stakeTime contamination
- accStakeTime doesn't respect epoch boundaries
- Major refactor needed: move to Synthetix-style implicit checkpointing
