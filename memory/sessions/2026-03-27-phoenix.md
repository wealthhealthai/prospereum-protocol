### Prospereum v3.2 Protocol Design — FINALIZED
After ~10 days of design discussion with Shu (co-founder) and Jason, the Prospereum protocol was fundamentally redesigned. All decisions are locked.

**Core changes from v2.3:**
- Reward metric: cumS high-water-mark ratchet (`cumS(t) = max(S_eco(t), cumS(t-1))`) replaces gross cumBuy
- Anti-compounding: `effectiveCumS = cumS - cumulativeRewardMinted` (Shu's elegant formula — no provenance tagging)
- Anti-spam: initial buy earns zero reward (S(N) is baseline only); first reward requires cumS(M) > S(N)
- CustomerVault: new contract, partner deploys for customers, customers are blockchain-agnostic
- S_min: $500 USDC minimum vault creation buy (USDC-denominated, not PSRE)
- Tier multipliers corrected: 0.8×/1.0×/1.2× → 8%/10%/12% effective rates
- Vault expiry: 52 epochs (~1 year)
- Enterprise vault management: NOT in protocol (WealthHealth Olympus builds service layer)
- Vault bond + vesting + distribution reward: all ELIMINATED
