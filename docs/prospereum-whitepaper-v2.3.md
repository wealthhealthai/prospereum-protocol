1. Abstract 
Prospereum is a decentralized behavioral mining protocol that aligns economic demand with 
progressive scarcity. 
Unlike Proof-of-Work (Bitcoin) or Proof-of-Stake (Ethereum), Prospereum introduces: 
Proof of Net Economic Contribution 
 
Token issuance is unlocked only when provable on-chain demand for PSRE is generated, and 
issuance is permanently constrained by an asymptotic scarcity function tied solely to emitted 
supply. 
Prospereum is neutral infrastructure. It does not encode moral judgment. Applications define 
aligned economic activity. The protocol measures only net acquisition and time-locked 
participation. 
 
2. Vision 
Bitcoin demonstrated decentralized scarcity. 
Ethereum demonstrated programmable coordination. 
Prospereum unifies both principles: 
• Scarcity discipline 
• Behavioral alignment 
It is designed to: 
• Reward real economic contribution 
• Prevent inflationary farming 
• Eliminate discretionary mint authority 
• Transition naturally from growth incentives to scarcity dominance

5. Behavioral Mining Reward Emission Model 
5.1 Overview 

---
_Page 3_

Each partner participates in the Prospereum protocol by creating a PartnerVault, a dedicated on-
chain contract used to acquire and distribute PSRE tokens.  
When a partner purchases PSRE through its PartnerVault, the protocol records the amount of new 
net buy generated during that epoch. These purchases typically correspond to PSRE rewards that 
the partner may distribute to its customers or referral participants as part of its commerce or 
incentive program. 
At the end of each epoch (defined as seven days duration), the protocol calculates the total reward 
budget based on the new net buy of PSRE generated by all partners and the protocol’s scarcity 
constraints. The reward budget is then divided into two pools: a partner reward pool and a staker 
reward pool. Partners receive rewards based on the amount of new net buy they generated during 
the epoch, adjusted by their partner status tier (bronze, silver, gold), while participants who lock 
PSRE tokens (including liquidity providers) receive rewards proportional to their time-weighted 
stake during the epoch. 
Once the epoch is finalized, the protocol automatically mints the required PSRE (subject to the 
emission reserve limit) and allocates the partner rewards to each PartnerVault. Staking rewards are 
distributed to the staking vault and can be claimed by participants. This process repeats every 
epoch, ensuring that token issuance is tied directly to verifiable on-chain economic activity while 
remaining bound by the protocol’s long-term scarcity model.

7. Governance 
The Prospereum protocol is governed by a decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) 
responsible for maintaining operational parameters while preserving the protocol’s core monetary 
policy. 

---
_Page 8_

The DAO may adjust certain parameters within predefined bounds in order to adapt to ecosystem 
growth and changing participation dynamics. 
7.1 Adjustable Parameters 
The DAO may adjust the following parameters: 
Base Reward Rate 
The base partner reward rate may be adjusted within the following range: 
0.05 ≤ 𝛼𝑏𝑎𝑠𝑒 ≤ 0.15 
This parameter controls the maximum percentage of new net buy that may be returned to partners 
as rewards. 
 
Partner Tier Thresholds 
The contribution-share thresholds used to determine partner tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold) may be 
adjusted. 
These thresholds determine the partner’s reward rate tier but do not affect the overall emission 
budget. 
 
Reward Pool Allocation 
The allocation of the epoch emission budget between partners and stakers may be adjusted within 
bounded limits. 
Default allocation: 
• 70% to partners 
• 30% to stakers 
The DAO may adjust this split within the range: 
60% ≤ 𝐵𝑝𝑎𝑟𝑡𝑛𝑒𝑟𝑠 ≤ 80% 
20% ≤ 𝐵𝑠𝑡𝑎𝑘𝑒𝑟𝑠 ≤ 40% 
The two pools must always sum to 100%. 
 
Initial Weekly Emission Ceiling 
The parameter 𝐸0, which determines the maximum weekly emission when the emission reserve is 
untouched, may be adjusted within predefined bounds. 
This allows the protocol to tune early-stage emission rates while maintaining the long-term scarcity 
curve.

The immutable supply cap, scarcity function, and emission reserve ensure that Prospereum’s 
monetary policy remains predictable and resistant to discretionary inflation. 
Governance is therefore limited to adjusting operational parameters while the fundamental token 
issuance model remains fixed.
